We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Communication: To send important updates, notifications, or respond to your inquiries
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve the app experience
  • Security: To protect against unauthorized access and maintain data security
  • Bug Fixes: To identify and resolve technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

Security Measures:
