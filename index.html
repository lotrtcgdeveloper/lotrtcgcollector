We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your LOTR TCG Collector account and associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to lotrtcgdeveloper@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request - LOTR TCG Collector"
  3. Include in your email:
    • The email address associated with your account
    • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account
  4. We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email once completed

What Data Will Be Deleted

The following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your email address
  • Your encrypted password
  • All trading card collection information
  • Any other personal information associated with your account

Data Retention

Once your deletion request is confirmed:

  • Immediate removal: Your account will be deactivated immediately
  • Complete deletion: All personal data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days
  • Backups: Data in backup systems will be removed within 90 days as backups are cycled
  • Legal obligations: We may retain certain data if required by law (e.g., for tax or legal purposes), but this is rare
